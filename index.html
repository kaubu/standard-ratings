Rating systems are all over the place

And they need to be standardized.

What model will we go with?

We'll base it on MyAnimeList's rating system, which uses adjectives instead of numbers. Here it is:

  1. Appalling
  2. Horrible
  3. Very Bad
  4. Bad
  5. Average
  6. Fine
  7. Good
  8. Very Good
  9. Great
  10. Masterpiece

But what if it uses a 5 star rating system?

Then, assuming it has a half step between stars, we'll use this:

  • Appalling
  • Horrible - ★
  • Very Bad
  • Bad - ★★
  • Average
  • Fine - ★★★
  • Good
  • Very Good - ★★★★
  • Great
  • Masterpiece - ★★★★★
